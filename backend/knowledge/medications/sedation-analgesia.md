# Sedation and Analgesia (General IR Context)

Sedation in IR is individualized to procedure type, patient comorbidity, and monitoring capability.

## Frequently Encountered Agents

- Benzodiazepines (for anxiolysis/sedation)
- Opioids (for analgesia)
- Local anesthetics
- Adjunct antiemetics and supportive agents

## Safety Themes

- Titrate to effect under monitoring
- Review respiratory risk and comorbidities
- Confirm fasting and medication plans pre-procedure
- Use institution-specific protocols
