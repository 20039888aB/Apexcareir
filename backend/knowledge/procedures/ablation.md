# Ablation in Interventional Radiology

Ablation treats selected lesions by thermal or non-thermal energy under image guidance.

## Common Modalities

- Radiofrequency ablation (RFA)
- Microwave ablation
- Cryoablation

## Preparation

- Lesion-focused imaging review
- Medication and bleeding-risk review
- Sedation/anesthesia planning

## Recovery

- Observation period and pain control
- Discharge instructions and return precautions
- Follow-up imaging to assess treatment response
