# Embolization Procedures

Embolization blocks selected blood vessels to control bleeding, shrink lesions, reduce abnormal flow, or support oncology pathways.

## Typical Indications

- Active hemorrhage
- Uterine fibroids (UFE)
- Prostatic enlargement (PAE)
- Tumor-directed arterial therapy (including TACE)
- Vascular malformations

## General Steps

1. Image-guided vascular access
2. Catheter navigation to target vessel
3. Controlled delivery of embolic material
4. Endpoint confirmation and post-procedure monitoring

## Risks

- Non-target embolization
- Pain or post-embolization syndrome
- Bleeding, infection, or contrast-related adverse effects
