# CIRSE Practice Themes

CIRSE patient and practice resources emphasize clear communication, consent support, and structured procedure explanations.

## Procedure Communication Framework

- What the procedure is
- Why it is being considered
- Expected benefits
- Risks and possible complications
- Preparation requirements
- Follow-up and recovery guidance

## Safety Themes

- Clarify alternatives and uncertainty
- Use plain language for patients
- Escalate emergencies immediately
- Avoid overpromising outcomes
