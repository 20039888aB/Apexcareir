# Core IR Devices and Consumables

Core categories include:

- Access needles and micropuncture kits
- Guidewires (hydrophilic and support wires)
- Catheters and microcatheters
- Introducer sheaths
- Balloons and stents/stent grafts
- Embolic materials (coils, particles, plugs, liquids)
- Drains and decompression catheters
- Biopsy needles and ablation probes

Device selection is anatomy- and indication-dependent.
