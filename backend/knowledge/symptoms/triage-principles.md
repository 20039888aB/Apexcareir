# Symptom Triage Principles

Symptoms alone rarely establish diagnosis. Clinical context, imaging, and labs are usually required.

## Ask Before Concluding

- Onset and duration
- Severity and progression
- Associated symptoms (fever, bleeding, breathlessness, neurologic deficits)
- Relevant history (surgery, trauma, cancer, clot risk, pregnancy)

## Red Flags

- Chest pain
- Difficulty breathing
- Severe or massive bleeding
- Sudden neurologic deficits
- Loss of consciousness
- Blue or cold limb

These require urgent medical evaluation.
