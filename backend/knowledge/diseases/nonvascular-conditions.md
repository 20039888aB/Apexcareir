# Non-vascular Conditions Relevant to IR

IR also supports non-vascular care in selected cases:

- Liver tumors and liver metastases
- Biliary obstruction
- Hydronephrosis and urinary obstruction
- Abscesses and complex fluid collections
- Pleural and peritoneal fluid disorders
- Benign gynecologic and prostate-related symptom syndromes

Interventions can include drainage, decompression, biopsy, and image-guided oncology procedures.
