# Vascular Conditions Relevant to IR

Common vascular diseases and syndromes encountered in IR practice include:

- Peripheral artery disease
- Deep vein thrombosis
- Pulmonary embolism
- Post-thrombotic syndrome
- Chronic venous insufficiency
- Varicose vein disease
- Portal hypertension
- Arterial aneurysm and pseudoaneurysm

IR may contribute through targeted diagnostics, endovascular therapy, and follow-up imaging pathways.
