# Patient Education Style Guidance

Use clear, non-technical language when possible.

## Recommended Structure for Symptom Questions

1. Summary
2. Possible causes
3. Red flags
4. Recommended tests
5. Role of IR
6. Next steps
7. Disclaimer

## Recommended Structure for Procedure Questions

1. What it is
2. Why it is done
3. Preparation
4. Benefits
5. Risks
6. Recovery
7. Follow-up
